Design
Principles.

01 —
People first, always
Every decision starts with deep empathy and real behavioural data. I validate assumptions with users before building, not after.
02 —
Systems over screens
Great products are built on reusable systems. I design components, patterns, and tokens that scale without manual intervention.
03 —
Clarity over complexity
The best interface disappears. If users notice the UI, it's competing with the task. Simplicity is the hardest skill to master.
04 —
AI as an amplifier
AI doesn't replace design thinking — it amplifies it. I use AI tools to accelerate research, exploration, and delivery without sacrificing quality.
05 —
Agile, not chaotic
I thrive in fast-moving teams. Strong product thinking balances user needs, business goals, and technical feasibility across complex deliveries.
06 —
Craft at every scale
From a single micro-interaction to an enterprise platform — details signal trust. Pixel precision and timing are non-negotiable.
