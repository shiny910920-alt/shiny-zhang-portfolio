01 — About the project

A calm space
for movement.

Halfmoon is an online yoga & fitness course platform where members can access guided courses easily — through the web or a companion mobile app, from anywhere.

The brief called for a design that felt clear and minimalist, yet still carried an atmosphere of inspiration, warmth, and stillness. The work spanned the full journey: defining the brand from scratch, building a visual identity and design system, then designing the web platform and mobile app on top of it.

Reflection

Designing a feeling,
not just a platform.

Halfmoon was an opportunity to own a project in its entirety — to carry a single idea from a blank page, through brand and identity, all the way into a working web and mobile product. That continuity is rare, and it let every decision stay true to one intention: calm.

The hardest part was restraint. A wellness platform can easily tip into either clinical minimalism or decorative excess. The work lived in the balance — a clear, minimal structure that still breathes warmth through colour, natural light, and an elegant editorial voice.

Running stakeholder workshops early meant the brand and the product grew from the same root, rather than being stitched together later. By delivering end-to-end, the result feels coherent — one system, one feeling, expressed faithfully from the logo to the last mobile screen.
